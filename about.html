About AgroGuru

Overview

AgroGuru is an innovative agriculture app designed to provide farmers with advanced tools and services to enhance their farming practices. Our app is dedicated to optimizing crop management and improving overall agricultural productivity.

Services

  • 1. Crop Rotation

    AgroGuru offers a smart Crop Rotation feature, allowing farmers to plan and manage crop rotations effectively. This helps in maintaining soil fertility and preventing pest and disease issues associated with continuous monoculture.

  • 2. Plant Disease Detection

    With our advanced technology, AgroGuru provides a Plant Disease Detection tool. This tool enables farmers to identify and address potential plant diseases early, preventing widespread crop damage and optimizing yield.

  • 3. Nutrient Monitoring

    AgroGuru's Nutrient Monitoring service allows farmers to track and manage the nutrient levels in their soil. This ensures that crops receive the necessary nutrients for healthy growth, leading to improved crop quality and yield.

  • 4. Logistics Tracking

    Efficient logistics are crucial in agriculture. AgroGuru provides a Logistics Tracking feature, helping farmers manage the transportation of crops, equipment, and resources. This ensures timely deliveries and reduces waste.

  • 5. Task Scheduler

    AgroGuru's Task Scheduler is a handy tool for farmers to plan and organize their daily tasks. This feature helps in streamlining operations, ensuring that critical tasks are completed on time, and maximizing productivity.
